Our Background

Who we are & What we do?



Formula ONE started as a small business of buying and selling laptops in 2014 with Facebook as its primary platform. Through persistent hard work and dedication, it grew and gained the attention not only of the customers but also the suppliers. What started out as a venture with secondhand laptops, we now offer an array of brand-new laptops from the biggest brands.

Formula ONE sets new standards in solution provision through our business tagline “Where affordability meets the quality”, using this approach we are able to cover all aspects of computing system that are needed without being confined to the restrictions, setting IT apart from all its competitors.

MISSION

To empower individuals and businesses through innovative technology solutions, providing seamless experiences that enhance productivity and improve the quality of life.

VISION

Our vision is to become the trusted and preferred destination for customers seeking the latest and most innovative laptop solutions, providing personalized support and an exceptional shopping experience that exceeds expectations.
